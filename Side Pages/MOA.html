Description

The major goal of the Scheme is a form of processing and preservation capacities and modernization and growth of obtainable food processing units with a vision to increase the level of processing, value addition leading to a diminution of wastage. It is a scheme of setting up modern abattoir under 5 ULBs viz. Krishnagar, Ranaghat, Nabadwip, Bhatpara and Contai are being approved with the objective to provide facility of scientific slaughtering and better hygienic environment. The scheme has envisaged setting up an abattoir for a capacity of slaughtering small animals, namely goat and sheep. The scheme is to provide civil construction of open lairage, covered lairage with truss structure, slaughtering unit, blood processing plant, effluent treatment plant, deep tube well etc. and necessary electro mechanical units.
